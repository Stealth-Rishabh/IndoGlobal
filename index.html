Indo Global College of Management

Indo Global College of Management and Technology (IGCMT) is one of the leading management institute of this region & is offering courses - BBA and BCA which are duly approved by UGC, Govt of Punjab and affiliated with IKG Punjab Technical University Jalandhar.
Indo Global College of Management and Technology provides world class infrastructure & facilities which includes spacious smart classrooms, tutorial rooms, Laboratories, Group Discussion rooms, Computer centre, well stocked library etc.

Program Offered

  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)

Eligibility

All those candidates who have passed 10+2 or its equivalent examination in any stream conducted by recognised Board/University/Council.
